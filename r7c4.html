Welcome to the General Hints and Tips Category
Below are some general points to acknowledge:
• Check appropriate apps such as NATs and UAV Forecast

• Choose and maintain a safe TOLA

• Complete paperwork intuitively

• Deconflict with other air operators prior to flight

• Do not disturb the pilot during flight unless there is risk to a drone or a pilot

• If other air operators enter your air space, yield to protect your drone

• Keep ‘eyes on’ at all times

• Keep all equipment safe and secure during flight

• Observe and log any warnings before, during and after flight

• The pilot is the person in charge and responsible for the flight

• Work within the parameters of ‘The Drone Code’
It is vital that these all of these points are acknowledged and adhered to where applicable.
