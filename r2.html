Considerations
Every MisPer is an individual and the stats are there as a guide.

Do not get hung up if things differ from the information provided.

Different organisations prefer different statistics but all are roughly similar.

Not all Missing Persons fit nicely into one specific category and your search tactics must consider this.

Conditions and environments may impact the statistics and also consider the impact of other persons in the area, causing some of the statistics to be pushed to the upper limits due to this footfall.
