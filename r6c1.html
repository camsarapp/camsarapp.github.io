Welcome to the Briefing Considerations Category
Below are some important points for all members to consider prior to deployment:
• Ensure that you know which team you are in and who else is in that team

• Ensure that you know the MisPer details and reasons for missing

• Understand your tasking

• Make sure that you have the relevant kit and equipment to fulfil your tasking

• Make sure that you have the correct mapping and paperwork for your tasking

• Ensure that your personal kit is sufficient and serviceable

• Consider PPE requirements

• Consider foods and fluids for the duration of your tasking

• Communication checks are to be made before deployment

• Confirm that all members are ready to deploy

• Ask and answer any questions before deployment

• If specialist taskings are required then ensure that the correct PPE is available and used where required
Briefing is a time for all members to be informed of the parameters of the search and the requirements of their tasking.
