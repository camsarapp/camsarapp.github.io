Welcome to the Risk Assessment Category
Below are some questions you should ask yourself when operating around water:
• How deep is the water?

• How easy is it to fall in?

• How easy is it to get out?

• What are the consequences?

• How fast is the water flowing?

• What are the exposure dangers?

• What are the obvious and unobvious hazards?

• How cold is the water and the air temperature?
Below are some dynamic considerations:
• What is the time?

• Can the water conditions change soon?

• What experience do I have with the available personnel?

• What are the conditions like now and what does a reliable forecast say?
Operating around water presents many risks and so a high degree of caution is required.
