Welcome to the Water Terminology Category
Below are some important terms:
Upstream/Upriver - this is the direction from where the flow of the river is coming from

Downstream/Downriver - this is the direction in which the river is flowing

River left - this is the bank area to your left with upstream behind you and downstream before you

River right - this is the bank area to your right with upstream behind you and downstream before you
