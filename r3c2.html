Welcome to the Pacing Category
Each person has a different size ‘pace’ when they walk.
To assist in navigation, we measure how many paces (double steps or every left or right foot count) an individual takes to walk 100m.

We can then easily convert this pace count to distances.

It is easiest if you round to the nearest pace count for calculations.

Ascending, descending, terrain types and underfoot conditions can affect your pace count.
