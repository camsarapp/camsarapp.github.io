Welcome to the Echo Codes Category
Echo codes are used to ensure patient confidentiality over an open radio network.
If specific personal information is required, consider a mobile phone.
Below are eight echo codes and their corresponding meanings:
Echo - MisPer/Casualty located

Echo Alpha - MisPer Uninjured

Echo Bravo - Minor injuries; team dealing

Echo Charlie - Critical-LRFR deploy/Additional team

Echo Delta - MisPer deceased/Crime scene

Echo Echo - MisPer located but team backing off

Echo Foxtrot - Require police presence immediately

Echo No Duff - Team member injured
Priority Echo calls instigate radio silence.
