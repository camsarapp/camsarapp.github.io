Considerations
Do not allow mission creep to take over your flight plan.

Your flight plan and records are just as important as the flight itself.

Weather apps and resources are a useful guide but be aware of their inaccuracies.

Record your entire flight and ensure that you review the footage to improve the next flight.

Always be aware of outside influences and their impact on the pilot as they could impact the safety of the flight.
