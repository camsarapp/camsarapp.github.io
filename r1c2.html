Welcome to the Phonetic Alphabet Category
The NATO phonetic alphabet is used over radio or telephone communication and ensures that letters can be understood in less-than-ideal circumstances, such as in an area with poor connection.
It was derived by international agencies that assigned code words to each letter of the alphabet acrophonically.

The NATO phonetic alphabet is great for communicating things like postcodes by voice.
